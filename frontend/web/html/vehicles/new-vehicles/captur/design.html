    Design

    DESIGN

    Fun and sporty, Captur is inspired by the concept car of the same name.

    FUN AND SPORTY DESIGN

    The 'Captur' Concept car that inspired the crossover of the same name invited us to 'Explore the world together'. With its fun and sporty looks, Captur adopts the same style as its predecessor:

    • Large wheels set in generous wheel arches for a sporty but sturdy look.
    • Roof decals that mimic the original shape of the concept car's roof.
    • Chunky dashboard design.
    • A new 'Arizona' orange body colour, warm and vibrant

    SUPERBLY PRACTICAL

    Let yourself be swept away by the styling of the Captur, the crossover from Renault. Inspired in name and in spirit by the Captur concept car, it has the looks and features of an urban hatchback.
    Superbly practical. Proudly expressing the new Renault identity.

    • Soft, flowing, balanced lines revealing a personality that is strong but not showy.
    • Compact dimensions (4.12m x 1.77m, 1.57m), high clearance (200 mm), large wheels (657 mm), bold side protection.
    • A colourful passenger compartment for a welcoming, warm and relaxed feel.
    • Affordable exterior and interior personalisation options. Captur combines the original two-tone paintwork with a wide variety of contrasting colour combinations, shades and designs.
