    Design

    DYNAMIC STYLING

    GET EXCITED ABOUT STYLE

    Renault Clio inherits the styling and emotion of DeZir, the concept car presented at the Paris Motor Show in October 2010.

    Personalisation

    Choose a car that's right for you

    Spoil yourself, with a broad range of personalised options to satisfy your every desire! Easy to mix and match, the personalisation options are applied to a range of exterior and interior features. Stand out from the crowd at the wheel of your very own Clio!

    EXTERIOR LOOKS

    With Clio, choose exactly the car you want, by selecting your own roof stickers, door mirror housings, grille trim strip, rear bumper and side protections. Treat yourself with one of the four 'Exterior Look' packs, enhancing your Clio with glossy black and the features below:

    • 4 different finishes for exterior trim strips: Chrome, Black, Red or Ivory.
    • 2 exterior roof stickers: 'Dots' and 'Sport'.
    • Black, Ivory, Red, Blue or Grey 17" diamond-cut  Desire Alloy wheels.

    Interior Options

    Personalise and adapt the interior ambience of your Clio Dynamique S from the dashboard, the door panels to the upholstery. All in three different colours: Black, Red and Blue.
    The steering wheel, armrest handles, gear lever base and air vent surrounds are also available in several colours on both Dynamique and Dynamique S versions (depending on gearbox), which are built into the parts directly using 'in mould' technology.

    CUSTOMISE R-LINK

    Personalise your homepage and favourites pages with the R-Link multimedia system. Still want more? Personalise your vehicle down to the engine sound with the R-Link 'R-Sound Effect' application and simulate the sound of a V8 engine responding to every acceleration!
