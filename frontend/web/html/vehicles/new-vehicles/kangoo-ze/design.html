    Design

    DESIGN

    The design of Kangoo Z.E. is different to that of the Kangoo. Show your environmental commitment by driving an electric van! At the front, Kangoo Z.E. features black headlamp surrounds, with a blue chrome Renault logo and strips. For an additional touch of style, opt for the two-tone bumper with satin-finish chrome.

    KANGOO MAXI Z.E. 5 SEATS

    KANGOO Z.E VERSION

    Discover the fully glazed 5-seater version of Kangoo Maxi Z.E! You can carry five people in comfort and also take advantage of a 1.3m3 loading area (extending up to 3.4m3 with the rear seat folded). Your Kangoo Maxi Z.E is no longer just an LCV!

    COMFORTABLE

    The 5-seater version of Kangoo Maxi Z.E. features the rear seating of the Kangoo leisure activity vehicle. Three rear seats and adjustable head restraints set high standards in comfort.

    MODULAR

    The 5-seater Kangoo Maxi Z.E. is a highly modular van! Opt for one of four different configurations depending on the number of passengers and the size of the objects to be carried. Do you need to load long, bulky objects? Just fold the rear seating to create a flat floor in one smooth movement!
